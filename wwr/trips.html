Our trips

Family Adventure on the Sereno River

Ideal for families and beginners, this trip offers a calm and scenic experience through calm waters, with the opportunity to enjoy nature and wildlife. Our expert guides ensure safety and fun for all ages.

Wild River Rapids Challenge

For adrenaline lovers, this trip will take you through exciting Class III and IV rapids. An action-packed experience that will test your skills and leave you with unforgettable memories. Previous experience or good physical condition is required.

Hidden Canyon Multi-Day Expedition

Immerse yourself in a multi-day adventure exploring remote canyons and camping under the stars. This trip combines challenging rapids with moments of serenity and exploration, offering total immersion in unspoiled nature.
