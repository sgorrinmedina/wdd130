History

In the heart of the rugged Colorado Rockies, nestled between pine-covered hills and the rushing waters of the Arkansas River, lies a small whitewater rafting company known as Eagle’s Descent Rafting, which eventually evolved into whitewater rafting adventures. Its story is one of passion, perseverance, and the unbreakable bond between people and the wild rivers they love.

It all began in 1972, when Jack Marlowe, a former U.S. Forest Service ranger with a deep love for the outdoors, first paddled the rapids near Buena Vista. Back then, the river was mostly explored by loggers and miners, not thrill-seekers. But Jack saw something more — adventure, challenge, and a way to connect people with nature’s raw power.
